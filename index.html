Manuel Peter Neuer

Neuer was born on March 27, 1986 in Germany and is now 35 years old. He started his career with the youth teams at Schalke 04. When he was five years old. On August 19, 2006, he played his first match in the German Bundesliga because he was too good. He won the DFB-Pokal and DFL-Ligapokal titles with Schalke 04 in 2011, He signed with Bayern Munich and has since won 26 trophies, including nine Bundesliga titles and two Champions League titles in 2013 and 2020. Neuer was named Germany's number one goalkeeper for the 2010 World Cup in South Africa. After a 0-0 draw with 1899 Hoffenheim, Neuer broke Bayern Munich's record for most consecutive competitive clean sheets, going over 1,000 minutes without conceding a sheet. He improved the record previously held by Oliver Kahn.
